I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the Alliance for Social, Political, Ethical, and Cultural Thought (ASPECT) at Virginia Tech.

My research focuses on Carceral Studies, Decolonial Theory, Critical Sociology, and Social Thought. Specifically, I examine the "carceral digital turn" and prisoner resistance using the American Prison Writing Archive (APWA). Drawing on the works of Hall, Fanon, and Marx, my work explores the intersection of racial capitalism, surveillance, and epistemic authority.
